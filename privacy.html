Privacy Policy for StoryIgnite Chrome Extension

Last Updated: June 03, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how StoryIgnite ("we", "us", or "our"), the developer of the StoryIgnite Chrome extension, collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our extension:

  • Usage Data: Automatically collected data such as genre selections and prompt generation frequency. This data is anonymized and not linked to individual users.
  • Local Storage Data: Favorite prompts saved locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your browser and is not accessed by us.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, or IP address) unless you voluntarily provide it through support requests.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the StoryIgnite extension.
  • To improve and personalize your experience by analyzing anonymized usage patterns.
  • To respond to support requests if you contact us.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share anonymized usage data with service providers to enhance the extension, subject to strict confidentiality agreements. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, including encryption of local storage data and secure handling of support communications. However, no online transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your local storage data by clearing Chrome extension data.
  • Opt-out of data collection by uninstalling the extension.
  • Contact us at support@storyignite.example.com for further assistance.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will be available within the extension or on our website. The "Last Updated" date reflects the latest revision. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
